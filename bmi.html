BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement of body fat based on height and weight that applies. BMI can be used to indicate if you are overweight, obese, underweight or normal.
A healthy BMI score is between 20 and 25. A score below 20 indicates that you may be underweight; a value above 25 indicates that you may be overweight.
